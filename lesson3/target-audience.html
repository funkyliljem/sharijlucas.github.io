Personas

Persona 1
Name: Brian Harris
Demographics:
    45 years old
    Married
    5 Children
    Bachelors degree in Business
Goals: Brian coaches his son's soccer team and needs up to date weather infomation so that he can plan for practice and games accordingly. Parents need plenty of notice when changes need to be made to the schedule due to the weather.
Persona 2
Name: Michelle Wagner
Demographics:
    21 years old
    Engaged
    College Student studying Medicine
    Enjoys outdoor activities and sports
Goals: Michelle is constantly outside. If she is not hiking, running or biking she is finding a quite place in nature to study. She likes things in her life planned out and organized. She likes to know well in advance what the weather will be so she can plan if she can workout outside or needs to find the gym. She also likes to plan her study time and if she can do it outside. Her and her fiancee are still trying to choose a place for the wedding which she would love to have outside.

Scenarios

    Will I still be able to hold soccer practice?
    Is it going to snow today?
    Can I plan a hiking activity for Saturday?
    What is the average tempertaure during July?
    Is my flight going to be cancelled?
